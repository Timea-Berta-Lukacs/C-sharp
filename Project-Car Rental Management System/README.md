A car rental application designed for individuals who do not own a personal vehicle and need one for daily activities.



Functionalities:

&#x09;•	After authentication, users are directed to the application where they can view available cars, register with their personal details, and create reservations under their name for a selected vehicle.

&#x09;•	After authentication, the administrator is directed to the application where they can view, add, and delete records related to cars, clients, and reservations made by users.
